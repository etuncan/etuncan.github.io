Who

A currently freelance web-developer with several years of professional work under his belt, Erin Tuncan is the creator behind Kit & Crow Creations. Born in Missouri in the mid-nineties, he has spend over half of his life living and working in Omaha, Nebraska. Erin has a wonderful wife, as well as two pets; a dog and cat.

What

This is the Kit & Crow Creations website, The umbrella under which I do freelance web-design and development. business run and operated by Erin Tuncan. It's purpose is to function as a portfolio as well as an adversitement for potential clients.
