Mission Statement

The purpose of this website is two-fold; to showcase the cohesive and "real world" application of knowledge pertaining to web-development and web-page coding/design. This is undertaken in several parts. The first aspect, design, is meant to show the ability to provide a visually appealing, cohesive, clean, and user friendly design. One that highlights the key aspects of each individual page while maintaining a common theme.
